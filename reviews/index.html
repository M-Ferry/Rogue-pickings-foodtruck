He's super funny and tries to keep customers entertained while waiting which is quite unique in the food truck world. I've had the falafel once before - but the Greek green goddess salad is now my personal preference it was very delicious! Thanks for the tastes and the entertainment!

Lara S.

I don't typically write reviews unless I feel moved. I first tried Rogue Pickings when I was just walking outside of East End Brewing company after a brewery tour. Stephen kindly approached me and asked if I'd like to try a new dish he just created. I'm from California, so we eat, breathe, live tacos over there. Sometimes our standards cause us to be skeptical of other type of food that is unusual or considered new. However, from this food truck, I was pleasantly surprised. Rogue Pickings food is unique and fresh. The chicken was shredded and was well complemented by the salsa, which was different from what I'm used to. Ingredients were very fresh with its own mild kick. It definitely hit the spot after knocking back a few brews. I feel like Stephen's generous character translates into his unique and creative cuisine. It doesn't hurt they sell beer as well. Anyway, point of this post is that Rogue Pickings food hits the spot everytime! And is perfect to have with a beer! Thanks, Rogue Pickings!
